About Me

Bonjour! I'm Christine.

I am an HR professional with over 20 years of experience navigating the modern corporate world, particularly within the entertainment industry. My journey has been shaped by my steadfast belief that employees are more than just numbers—they are individuals with unique needs, aspirations, and talents.

Sadly, authentic leadership and empathy are often missing in today’s corporate environment. This gap has driven my passion for corporate coaching. My mission is simple yet profound: to transform the corporate world one human at a time.
